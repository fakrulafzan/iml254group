Return & Refund Policy

1) Items can be returned or exchanged within 3 days of receipt.
2) Sale items, as-is items, defective sale items, and promotional-priced items are non-returnable, non-refundable, and non-exchangeable.
3) Exchanges depend on stock availability.
4) Refunds are not allowed; only exchanges are permitted.
5) Returned items must be in original condition: unworn, unaltered, unwashed, un-ironed, with all tags and original packaging.
6) Returned goods will be inspected upon receipt. Items not in original condition will be returned to the customer via JNT pay at drop off.
7) Customers are responsible for shipping charges on exchanged items.
8) Exchanges can be made for a different product of equal or lesser value (no refunds for the difference).
9) Items cannot be exchanged if they:
- Are damaged (torn, stained, wrinkled, smell of cigarette or perfume)
- Have been washed, altered, or ironed
- Have stains (makeup, food, pen, etc.)
- Are defective due to customer actions
- Are returned without an invoice or receipt
- Are returned more than 3 days after receipt
- Are returned without price tags or labels
